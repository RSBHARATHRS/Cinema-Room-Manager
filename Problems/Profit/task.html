Profit

Ann puts \( M \) money in the bank. The bank increases Ann's deposit by \( P \) percent every year. Ann wants to know how many years should pass until her deposit in the bank reaches \( K \) money. Can you help her to answer this question?

The input contains three integers \( M, P, K \). It is guaranteed that all numbers are positive and \( K \geq M \).
Output the answer to Ann's question.


Sample Input:
1 100 8

Sample Output:
3


Sample Input:
100 15 120

Sample Output:
2



Memory limit: 256 MB
Time limit: 8 seconds
